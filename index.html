Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

An exam voucher is a unique alphanumeric code that you can use to pay for your exam fee on the official booking website instead of using a credit card. It acts as a 100% pre-paid token.

Validity depends on the exam. PTE vouchers are typically valid for 11 months, while AWS and GRE vouchers have a validity of 6-12 months. We always mention the exact validity before purchase.

While booking your exam on the official portal, proceed to the checkout/payment page. There will be an option to "Enter Voucher Code" or "Promo Code". Enter the code we provided and your total payable amount will become zero.

Once a voucher is issued, it cannot be refunded or exchanged, as it is a unique digital asset. However, if a voucher is proven to be invalid (which is extremely rare), we provide a 100% replacement or refund.
