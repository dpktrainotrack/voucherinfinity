This policy explains how Voucher Infinity handles personal information when you use our website or contact our team.

1. Overview

Voucher Infinity respects your privacy and is committed to handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect, why we collect it, and the choices available to you.

2. Information we may collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as when you contact us, submit an enquiry, or make a purchase. This can include:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Information relevant to your requested exam voucher or order.
  • Messages and other details you choose to send to our support team.
  • Basic technical information, such as device, browser, and website usage data.

3. How we use information

We use information to provide and improve our services, respond to enquiries, process requests, communicate about orders, help prevent misuse, and meet applicable legal obligations. We only use information where there is a valid reason to do so.

4. When information may be shared

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers when needed to operate the website, complete a service you request, or comply with law. These parties are expected to protect information and use it only for the relevant service.

5. Security and retention

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to safeguard personal information. No online system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. We retain information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including record-keeping and legal requirements.

6. Your choices

You may ask to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law. You may also opt out of non-essential promotional communications at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe option in a message, where available.

7. Contact us

Questions about your privacy?

Our team is happy to help with requests or questions about this policy.
